How To Make The Most Delicious Hot Chocolate!

Do you know that feeling when you just wanna cuddle up on the couch and watch a movie with a nice, cozy winter drink? Well I do! To make it happen, I tested out many different recipes for the best hot chocolate out there and found many! So, I combined the recipes I found and made a new one!

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/3 cup of boiling water
  • 3 and 1/2 cups of milk
  • 3/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of half and half

    • Instructions:

    1. First, add the dry ingredients to a pan and whisk in boiling water.
    2. Then, bring it to a simmer and cook for 2 minutes, stirring it constantly in the process.
    3. Stir in the milk and turn of the heat.
    4. Next, add in some vanilla extract to the pan.
    5. Finally, add the half and half to a cup and add the hot chocolate on top.
    6. It's optional but you can add some marshmellows and garnish it with some whipped cream and a pinch of cinnamon.

      7. And there you have it! The best hot chocolate recipe! I hope you try it out and enjoy it! Happy Winter!
